ABOUT ME

Hi, I am a software developer who is constantly pushing myself to grow and build new things. Recently, however, I've decided to apply that same curiosity to the visual world by picking up a camera. I absolutely love traveling, and this site serves as the home for that adventure. Thank you for following along as I explore new places and new perspectives!
